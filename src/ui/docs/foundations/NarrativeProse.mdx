# Narrative Prose

This is the surface where the story actually happens — the `.chat-prose` layer in
`theme.css`, rendered by `ChatMessage` for every Game Master turn. It is the clearest
expression of **"serif for story, sans for system":** the UI recedes, and the prose
glows in Spectral.

Markdown is parsed and re-styled into a small **roleplay vocabulary** so a turn reads
like a page from a book, not a chat log.

## The roleplay vocabulary

<Stage>
  <div style={{ maxWidth: 640 }}>
    <ChatMessage
      isUser={false}
      content={`The tavern door groans shut behind you. *Rain drips from your cloak onto the warped floorboards.* A hooded figure in the corner looks up.

"You're late," she says, not unkindly. "Sit. We have little time."

**She's measuring you — every word a test.**`}
    />
  </div>
</Stage>

- **`"Dialogue"`** → quoted text becomes bright `rp-dialogue` (parchment-50) — spoken words stand out.
- **`*Action*`** → italics become muted `rp-action` (parchment-300) — stage directions recede.
- **`**Thought**`** → bold becomes arcane-tinted `rp-thought` (arcane-300), wrapped in parentheses — the unspoken.
- Everything else is narrative body in **Spectral** at a comfortable reading measure.

## Player vs. Game Master

The player's turn is an **ember candlelit bubble** in the UI sans; the Game Master's
turn is serif prose. The contrast is deliberate — *you* speak in the present, the world
answers in story.

## Markdown

Headings mark scene breaks; lists offer choices; blockquotes and inline `code` carry
emphasis and artifacts. All re-styled to the candlelit theme.

<Stage>
  <div style={{ maxWidth: 640 }}>
    <ChatMessage
      isUser={false}
      content={`## The Sunken Library

You descend into the \`archive\`. The water is *waist-deep* and cold, and the ink still moves.

> Somewhere below, a page turns on its own.

You could:
- Light the lantern
- Follow the ink-trail
- Turn back while you still can`}
    />
  </div>
</Stage>

> Implementation: `ChatMessage` maps each markdown node to a `.markdown-*` / `.rp-*`
> class and splits `"quoted"` spans into `rp-dialogue`. The classes live in the
> `@layer components` block of `src/ui/styles/theme.css`.
