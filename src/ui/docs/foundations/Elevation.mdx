# Elevation & Shadows

Two systems combined: soft **dark ambient shadows** (`shadow-sm/md/lg/xl`) for
lift, plus **candlelight glow** (`shadow-glow-ember`, `shadow-glow-arcane`) for
focus and "alive" elements. Cards are surface-lighten + a hairline border + a
soft shadow — *not* heavy material elevation.

<ShadowScale />

- **`shadow-md`** is the default card lift.
- **`shadow-glow-ember`** rings interactive/active elements in warm candlelight; **`shadow-glow-arcane`** does the same in violet for AI moments.
- **`shadow-card-hover`** combines elevation + ember glow in a single box-shadow — it's what an interactive `Card` animates to on hover.
