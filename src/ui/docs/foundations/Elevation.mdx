# Elevation & Shadows

Two systems combined: soft **dark ambient shadows** (`shadow-sm/md/lg/xl`) for
lift, plus **candlelight glow** (`shadow-glow-ember`, `shadow-glow-arcane`) for
focus and "alive" elements. Cards are surface-lighten + a hairline border + a
soft shadow — *not* heavy material elevation.

<ShadowScale />

- **`shadow-md`** is the default card lift.
- **`shadow-glow-ember`** rings interactive/active elements in warm candlelight; **`shadow-glow-arcane`** does the same in violet for AI moments. The **`-strong`** twins push the glow for hero moments; the **`-soft`** twins are the quiet resting halo on tinted glyph plates (`IconTile`).
- **`shadow-ring-ember` / `shadow-ring-arcane`** are the `Avatar` identity rings — a solid ring + halo marking the player (ember) or the AI (arcane).
- **`shadow-input-focus`** is the field-level focus glow (the form twin of the global focus ring); **`shadow-card-hover`** combines elevation + ember glow in a single box-shadow — what an interactive `Card` animates to on hover (`-arcane` for AI-side cards).
- **`shadow-glow-verdant` / `shadow-glow-blood`** are tonal elevations — a semantic hue at emphasis alpha — used by `Toast` so a success or error notice carries its own coloured lift.
