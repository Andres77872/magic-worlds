# Logo

The mark is a **crescent moon cradling an ember** — *dreams (the crescent
reverie) lit by the spark of a story (the ember)*. The wordmark pairs the
emblem with "Reverie" set in Cormorant Garamond.

## Usage rules

- **Clear space** — keep padding around the logo at least equal to the emblem's height.
- **Never recolor the ember** to a non-brand hue. The flame gradient (`ember-300 → ember-500`) is fixed.
- **Dark grounds only.** The logo is built for `ink-800` (canvas) or darker. Don't place it on light or busy backgrounds.
- Prefer the **mark** alone for tight spaces (favicon, avatar, app rail) and the **wordmark** for headers and marketing.

> The SVGs live in `src/assets/brand/` (`logo-mark.svg`, `logo-wordmark.svg`) and
> inherit the ember palette, so they stay in sync with the design tokens.
